# Happy Farm 2026 Season Timeline

> **Season Start:** February 1, 2026
> **First Planting:** February 15-20, 2026

## Season Overview

### Preparation Phase (Feb 1-14)
- Soil preparation and amendments
- Irrigation system check
- Seed inventory and ordering
- Seedling setup (if starting indoors)
- Tool maintenance

### Early Season (Feb 15 - Mar 31)
**Target:** First seeds/seedlings in ground Feb 15-20

**Cool Season Crops** (can handle light frost):
- Leafy greens (lettuce, spinach, kale)
- Brassicas (broccoli, cauliflower, cabbage)
- Root vegetables (carrots, beets, radishes)
- Alliums (onions, garlic sets)
- Peas

**Notes:**
- Start heat-loving crops indoors (tomatoes, peppers)
- Monitor for late frosts
- Succession planting for continuous harvest

### Mid Season (Apr 1 - Jun 30)
**Focus:** Warm season transplants and direct seeding

**Warm Season Crops** (after last frost ~mid-March):
- Tomatoes (transplant)
- Peppers (transplant)
- Squash family (zucchini, cucumbers, melons)
- Beans
- Corn

**Notes:**
- Harvest early cool season crops
- Begin succession plantings
- Monitor irrigation as temperatures rise

### Peak Season (Jul 1 - Sep 30)
**Focus:** Harvest and succession planting

**Activities:**
- Peak harvest for summer crops
- Start fall crop seedlings
- Succession plantings for continuous production
- Soil maintenance between crops

### Fall Season (Oct 1 - Nov 30)
**Focus:** Fall crops and season wind-down

**Fall Plantings** (if applicable):
- Second round of cool season crops
- Winter cover crops

**Activities:**
- Harvest late summer crops
- Soil amendments and cover cropping
- Tool cleaning and storage prep

### Winter (Dec 1 - Jan 31)
**Focus:** Planning and rest

**Activities:**
- Season evaluation
- Next season planning
- Seed ordering for following season
- Infrastructure improvements
