---

## Legend

### Water Needs (for **established** plants)

- **High**: needs consistently moist soil; quality drops fast with dry swings
- **Medium**: regular irrigation; tolerates mild dry intervals
- **Low**: drought-tolerant once established (still water for germination)

### Planting Windows (Zone 10a shorthand)

- **Cool-season prime**: **Sep–Feb** (shoulders Aug & Mar)
- **Warm-season prime**: **Mar–Jun** (some extend to Aug with shade/water)
