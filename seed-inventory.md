## Notes

### Seasonal Definitions (Rough)

- **Cool Season**: Plant fall through early spring, tolerates frost
- **Warm Season**: Plant after last frost, needs warm soil
- **Perennial**: Plant once, returns yearly (or self-seeds)

### Packet Types

- **Single**: One variety per packet
- **Mixed**: Multiple varieties or colors in one packet
