# **Happy Farm Volunteer Team Member**

## **Deer Park Monastery**

### **Mindful Farming**

The Deer Park Happy Farm is a living expression of our love and respect for Mother Earth. Through our mindful cultivation, we aspire to re-establish healthy relationships between human communities and the earth, offering nourishment for body, mind, and spirit while establishing regenerative practices for future generations.

We are seeking **Volunteer Team Members** to join our community. This community role integrates vegetable growing with mindfulness practice, nourishing both the earth and the spiritual growth of the community.

### **Overview**

As a Farm Team Member, and Core Lay Community Member, you will contribute to the community through daily practice sessions and presence in the community. During working periods you will be able to help steward the daily operations of our 36-bed vegetable plot while embodying the principles of mindful working. Living and practicing in community with other Farm Team members, lay residents, and monastics, you will help to guide the healthy production of organic vegetables that provide for our community meals. You will support a living classroom for mindfulness and community building rooted in sustainable agriculture.

### **Core Responsibilities**

-   Participate in daily meditation practices, Sunday and Thursday Days of Mindfulness, and community meals

-   Maintain good communication and connection with all farm participants, including: volunteers, visitors, neighbors, monastics and school of interbeing members.

-   Model mindful harmonious working that integrates contemplative awareness with practical skill

-   Support farm-based learning opportunities for retreatants and volunteers

-   Assess plant health, irrigation systems and soil health.

-   Implement processes and interventions for plant health and productivity

-   Develop planting schedules, succession planning, and harvest timing.

-   Maintain farm logs, tools, and infrastructure

<!-- -->

-   Support composting systems, including *humanure* composting protocols

-   Guide the farm toward closed-loop, regenerative growing practices

### **Qualifications**

**Farm Experience:**

-   Minimum 2 years of hands-on organic farming experience

-   Knowledge of organic growing methods, pest management, and soil health preferred

-   Experience with small-scale diversified vegetable production preferred

-   Familiarity with irrigation systems and farm infrastructure preferred

**Practice Experience:**

-   Minimum 1 year of practice experience in the Plum Village tradition

-   Commitment to living according to the Five Mindfulness Trainings

-   Commitment to working with humanure and existing compost systems

-   ***Required: Attendance at the upcoming Rains Retreat at Deer Park Monastery. All potential candidates must attend this retreat which begins October 17. Candidates must satisfy Solidity Hamlet Housing Requirements: Male, Assigned-Male-At-Birth, in a Male-Female, or Male-Male partnership.***

**Personal Qualities:**

-   Ability to work as part of a collaborative team and follow the lead of others.

-   Strong observational skills and attention to detail

-   Strong communication skills for coordinating with diverse community members

-   Physical capacity for outdoor agricultural work in Southern California climate

-   Enthusiasm for integrating contemplative practice with farming

**Duration:** One-year commitment with possibility of renewal, *following successful completion of the Rains retreat*. **Housing:** shared dormitory rooms within Solidity Hamlet community. **Schedule:** Integration with monastery daily schedule and agricultural seasonal rhythms **Compensation:** Housing, meals. **This is a volunteer role. Successful completion of the Rains Retreat *[does not guarantee]{.underline}* acceptance to the team.**
