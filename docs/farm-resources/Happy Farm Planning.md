## **GENTLE CARE CONSIDERATIONS**

### **Watering Approach:**

-   **Daily attention**: New seeds will likely need daily moisture in this heat

-   **Deep watering rhythm**: Established plants might prefer thorough watering every 2-3 days

-   **Morning timing**: Generally easier on plants and reduces disease risk

-   **Mulching**: Heavy mulch can help retain moisture and keep soil cooler

### **Heat Management Ideas:**

-   **Shade cloth**: 30-50% shade during extreme heat waves could help

-   **Row covers**: Temporary protection when temperatures spike

-   **Transplant timing**: Evening planting tends to be less stressful

### **Nursery Space Daily Rhythms:**

-   **Morning check**: Brief walk-through, maybe 5 minutes

-   **Debris awareness**: Keeping eucalyptus leaves swept away

-   **Gentle watering**: Check container moisture levels

-   **Safety awareness**: Watch for falling branches
