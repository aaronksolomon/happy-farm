# Drip Line Tensioner Team Task Breakout

**Event:** Happy Farm Spring Opening
**Date:** February 28, 2026
**Team Lead:** AWU Volunteer
**Team Size:** 5-6 people

---

## Overview

This team makes drip line tensioner weights and dowels. Target: supplies for 8 rows.

---

## Materials Needed

- Burlap
- Gravel
- Old cotton (optional liners)
- Eucalyptus branches (~1/2" diameter, 30" long, straight)

---

## Targets

- **24 weights** (3 per row x 8 rows)
- **16 dowels** (2 per row x 8 rows)

---

## Making Weights

1. Cut burlap into ~10" squares
2. Optional: line with old cotton
3. Fill with 2-3 cups of gravel
4. Fold up corners and tie off securely

---

## Making Dowels

1. Find straight eucalyptus branches (~1/2" diameter)
2. Cut to 30" length
3. Strip bark and clean

---

## Coordination Notes

**Seedling Team:** Once tensioners are ready, they can be used on drip lines in newly planted beds. Coordinate with Seedling Team on which rows need tensioners first.
